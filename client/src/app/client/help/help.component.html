How do I cancel my reservation for accommodation?

1. Go to Trips, then select the trip you want to cancel.
2.Click on Show more travel plans, then on Show details.
3.Click on Modify or Cancel.
4.Click on Cancel reservation.

How do I modify my reservation for an experience?

1. Go to Travel, then click on the experience for which you want to modify the reservation.
2. Click Modify or Cancel.
3.Click Next under what you want to change: Change date or time, Add travelers to booking, or Remove travelers from booking.
4.Enter the new information and, depending on the changes made, click Choose or Continue.
5.Verify the information, then click Confirm and Pay.

When will you pay for your reservation?

You have just made a reservation. Congratulation ! What are the next steps ? In all countries, your payment method will be debited as soon as your reservation is confirmed (with the exception of deposits). This also applies to instant bookings. Regardless of the date you make the reservation, the host does not receive payment until 24 hours after you enter the premises. This gives you and your host time to make sure everything is in order.

What happens if my traveler cancels?

If your traveler cancels their reservation, you will automatically receive a notification and your calendar will be updated to accommodate other travelers.

Decline a travel request

No one is ever safe from the unexpected! It is normal not always to be able to respond favorably to all your requests. No problem: here's how.
1.Reject the request before it expires within 24 hours: This is important for your response rate, and is one of the factors that will allow you to become a Superhost.
2. Decide to block these dates or open them for booking to other travelers.
3. Have you changed your mind? It happens ! All you need to do is ask travelers to submit a new request, or send them a special offer or invitation to book. You can also decline their request for information, which prevents travelers from submitting a reservation request.

Comments on experiences

At the end of an experience, travelers have 30 days to leave a public comment. They also have the option of sending their remarks to the host privately. Remember that travelers can write reviews even if they arrive late, leave early, or did not attend the experience because they could not find the meeting point. you.

How do I cancel a reservation as an Experience Host?

To cancel a time slot in your experience, select it in your calendar, then click Cancel experience. Travelers will receive notification and a full refund.
